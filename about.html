Who is Jovani?

I'm a 28 year-old current UX/UI student at Career Foundry with an expected graduation date of July 2019. I started off my journey learning how to code via Free Code Camp about 8 months ago. The more I learned how to build things with code, the more I became addicted with a deep interest on the front-end/visual aspect of design and development. Now I code everyday and look forward to it, it's actually the best part of my day besides learning something new. I'm always watching tutorials, listening to podcasts, reading articles, staying up on the latest updates, news, and trends. I'm very excited to see how far I've come and how far I'll go!

Background

Currently, I work as an administrative professional in health care. I have over 10 years of experience in a variety of customer service roles. I am a visual and creative individual who understands the importance of the user experience which has led me to my studies in UX Research and Design. I'm a very analytical person and love to solve problems! My background led me to searching for a career that was more challeneging on a day-to-day basis and allowed me to utlize these skills. I'm also pretty naturally curious, its common to hear me ask "why" and "how" often. I'm fascinated by psychology and human behaviors so I plan to take some college courses on Human Computer Interaction via Coursera in the future.


Current Languages: I'm very proficient in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and jQuery. I also know some Sass and basic Javascript and plan on mastering Javascript when I've graduated my UX course so that I can give it my full time and attention. The JS Frameworks I'm interested in learning are Angular, React and Redux.

My full background story is kind of lengthy and not your typical story but makes for a great conversation so if you're interested, just reach out and I'd be glad to tell you more in exchange for hearing your story. We've all got a story and it's intriguing to hear other people's journeys!
