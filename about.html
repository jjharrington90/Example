Who is Jovani?

I'm a 28 year-old current UX/UI student at Career Foundry. I started off learning how to code via Free Code Camp about a year ago. The more I learned how to build things with code, the more I became addicted. Now I code everyday and look forward to it, it's actually the best part of my day besides learning something new. I'm always watching tutorials, listening to podcasts, reading articles, staying up on the latest updates and news, etc. I'm very excited to see how far I've come and how far I'll go!

Currently, I work as an administrative professional in health care. I have over 10 years of experience in customer service roles. I'm a very visual and creative person who understands the importance of user experience hence the career switch. I'm looking to combine my experiences with my passion in a new role. I enjoy solving problems and I'm naturally curious, its common to hear me ask "why" and "how" often. I'm fascinated by psychology and human behaviors so I plan to take some college courses on Human Computer Interaction via Coursera in the future.

I know basic Javascript and jQuery so far and stopped there as I wanted to master CSS, Bootstrap and jQuery before turning all my focus and attention to Javascript. The JS Frameworks I'm interested in learning are Angular, React and Redux.

My full background story is kind of lengthy and not your typical story but makes for a great conversation so if you're interested, just reach out and I'd be glad to tell you more in exchange for hearing your story. We've all got a story and it's intriguing to hear other people's journeys!
